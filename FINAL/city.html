Puebla’s Special Economic Development Zone

Also known as ZEDE, the special economic development zone in Mexico’s capital CDMX is an interesting project and has allowed for the stimulation of economic development. ZEDE aims to be the ideal destination for the development of new and innovative investment projects. This zone will create opportunities which will allow for the expansion of production and the development of new products and services. In addition, it provides an ideal zone for value-added production. It will conveniently offer a single location that meets logistical needs while also providing cost reduction, international competitiveness, trade, and tax benefits. Due to a heavy reliance on its oil sector (where profits are directly linked to international oil prices), the government has developed this program to stimulate other sectors. By stimulating these sectors the government aims to not only stabilise the national economy but strengthen it, ensuring that there is not an over-reliance on a single sector. Puebla

The state of Puebla combines history, tradition, art, architecture and its traditional flavors. Located in the central zone of the country, it borders the states of Tlaxcala and Hidalgo to the north and Oaxaca to the south. It is one of the favorite destinations for travelers due to the beauty of its attractions and its unique cuisine. Puebla de Zaragoza is the capital and its largest city. Due to its cultural richness, it was declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. During your visit you will admire the architecture of its cathedral and colonial buildings, you cannot miss the International Museum of the Baroque, the Forts of Loreto and Guadalupe, try the mole, the chiles en nogada and the sweet potatoes. You can see the most beautiful landscapes in its Magical Towns. In Cuetzalan you will be surprised by its waterfalls, caves and mountains and you will learn about its traditions and rituals. Get in touch with the calm and provincial spirit of Chignahuapan, where you will find hot springs and learn about the art of making Christmas spheres, a typical activity in the region. Enjoy a family day among the streets of Atlixco, which is guarded by the Popocatépetl volcano. Find in Cholula the largest pyramid in Mesoamerica, its pre-Hispanic sanctuaries, as well as various colonial temples. Between ancestral traditions, nature and unique flavors, the state of Puebla awaits you. Source: puebla.com
