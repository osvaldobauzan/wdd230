Target Audience


Our target audience will be adults, owners of small and medium businesses who are interested in growing the economy of their community, people interested in growing their businesses, wanting to support other members of the community.



Personas


Mark

Occupation: Small business owner, dedicated to renovate old furniture

Demographics: Age: 34, makes about $15k per year with a side job. He has a family of 5 and loves spending time with them, they enjoy bowling as a family. He uses his phone primarily to promote his small business in social media

Goals: Mark wants to be able to leave his side job and establish his own shop, for that he needs to grow his small business with more help than social media


Monica

Occupation: Spa owner

Demographics: Age: 25, makes about $10k per year with the spa and she's finishing his masters degree, she mostly uses her computer for homework and her phone for contacting her clients

Goals: Monica needs to be able to comunicate with her clients in more efficient way so she can grow her business for when she finishes her degree
